# World Cup 2026 Team Tracker

A web-based platform to follow your assigned team's progress throughout the FIFA World Cup 2026. Track match results, standings, and receive email notifications for your team's games.

**Live Site:** https://sysqo82.github.io/World-Cup-2026/

---

## How to Use

### Registration & Login
1. Visit the website and click **Register** to create an account
2. Enter your name and email address
3. You'll be automatically assigned a random World Cup team
4. Check your email for a verification code to log in

### View Your Team's Progress
- **My Account**: See all your team's fixtures across all tournament stages
- **Group Stage**: View live group standings and match results
- **Knockout Rounds**: Follow your team's progress through Round of 16, Quarter Finals, Semi Finals, and the Final

### Email Notifications
- Receive automatic email updates when your team plays a match
- Toggle email notifications on/off in your account settings
- Change your registered email address anytime from your account page

### Navigation
Use the dropdown menu at the top of any page to quickly navigate between:
- Home
- Group Stage
- Round of 16
- Quarter Finals
- Semi Finals
- Third Place Playoff
- Final
- My Account
